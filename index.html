About the Author

Founder | Investor | Activist

Andrs Luna leads projects that transform how people live and connect in the digital age. He helped define the interoperability protocol between biometric data and blockchain infrastructure in underrepresented communities.


His public health infrastructure, Consensus.Center, benefits more than 800,000 patients from Indigenous, Black, and multi-ethnic communities in LATAM, directly confronting the global algorithmic bias crisis through his premise of free digital health in hard-to-reach regions.


Andrs was honored in 2025 alongside Colombias Vice Presidency and the Global Black Economic Forum at the 174th Anniversary of the Abolition of Slavery, and previously recognized from the US Embassy as Afrodescendent of the Year (2015).


Andrss track record as a serial tech entrepreneur includes securing funding on TV show Shark Tank (2019), speaking at TEDx Conference (2018), and selling a company to Selina Hospitality (SLNAF) in 2023.
